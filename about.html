About Us

  1. Home
  2. About

Trusted Ride Hailing Services in the Northern Ghana

Chale Ride is the premier ride-hailing platform designed to meet the unique transportation needs of Northern Ghana. Offering both car and tricycle (Tuk Tuk) options, Chale Ride ensures that every journey is comfortable, safe, and affordable. Our user-friendly app makes it easy to book a ride with just a few taps, allowing you to specify your current location and destination. Payment is flexible, with options to pay in cash or via Mobile Money, catering to diverse customer preferences.

Our drivers are thoroughly vetted and possess extensive local knowledge, guaranteeing reliable and efficient service. Available 24/7, Chale Ride is committed to providing transportation solutions at any time of day. We pride ourselves on punctuality, ensuring you reach your destination on time, every time. Our competitive pricing offers exceptional value for money without compromising on quality. With Chale Ride, you get more than just a ride; you experience a seamless, stress-free journey. Join the many satisfied customers who have made Chale Ride their go-to transportation service in Northern Ghana.
