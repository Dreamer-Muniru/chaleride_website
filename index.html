How It Works

Chale Ride allows you to easily book a car or tricycle (Tuk Tuk) by, inputing your location and destination, and pay with cash or Mobile Money. Chale Ride is the only app providing convenient and reliable ride-hailing experience in Northern Ghana.

About Chale Ride

Chale Ride was founded with a mission to make transportation accessible and reliable in Northern Ghana. Our platform connects riders with drivers seamlessly, offering a variety of vehicles to meet different needs. From daily commutes to special trips, Chale Ride ensures you reach your destination safely and on time.

  • We prioritize punctuality and safety in every ride

  • Competitive pricing to ensure value for money

  • Easy booking process and multiple payment options

  • Drivers with extensive knowledge of Northern Ghana
